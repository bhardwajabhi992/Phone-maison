Samsung Galaxy Note 20 smartphone was launched on 5th August 2020. The phone comes with a 6.70-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1080x2400 pixels and an aspect ratio of 20:9. As far as the cameras are concerned, the Samsung Galaxy Note 20 on the rear packs a 12-megapixel primary camera ith an f/1.8 aperture and a pixel size of 1.8-micron; a 64-megapixel camera with an f/2.0 aperture and a pixel size of 0.8-micron, and a 12-megapixel camera with an f/2.2 aperture and a pixel size of 1.4-micron.
